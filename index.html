About Ryan Borbone

I am a video game programmer from a small town in Massachusetts. In senior year in high school I decieded to combine my skills with my interests and ended up going to Becker College. Coming here I plan on recieving my Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Media Design with a Game Development and Programming Concentration.

I am now crusing through college and any free time put towards developing my own games, participating in game jams, and going to any conventions or events possible.

I've started programming here and there in high school and had started to take it serious in senior year. Through my middleschool to high school I had picked up other various skills such as 3D modeling, graphic design, sound design, and learning how to network.
